Privacy Policy

Chess Traps

Effective date: April 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Chess Traps ("the App"), developed by Simbre Achraf (also known as AchrafCyber), handles your information when you use the App on Android or iOS devices.

By using Chess Traps, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information we collect

Chess Traps does not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information. However, third-party services integrated into the App may collect data as described below.

The App itself does not require account creation, login, or submission of personal details.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

Chess Traps uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Usage data and interaction with ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: on Android via Settings → Google → Ads, and on iOS via Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Analytics — Firebase & Google Analytics

The App uses Firebase and Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the App. These services may collect:

  • App usage events (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • Device identifiers and technical specifications
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • General geographic region (country/city level)

This data is used solely to improve the App's performance and user experience. It is processed by Google in accordance with their data policies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Children's privacy

Chess Traps does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data retention

Since we do not directly collect personal data, we do not maintain user data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) is retained according to their respective data retention policies.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you by third-party services
  • Request deletion of your data from those services
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Disable analytics tracking via your device settings

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
